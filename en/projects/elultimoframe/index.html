El Último Frame

Podcast about Super Smash Bros. with interviews, specials and experiences from the Spanish-speaking Smash community.

About the podcast

El Último Frame was a podcast focused on the competitive Super Smash Bros. community in Spanish, featuring in-depth interviews with players, tournament organizers, content creators, and other key figures in the scene.

Platforms:

  • YouTube
  • Spotify
  • Apple Podcasts

My first step in content creation

El Último Frame was my first internet content creation project. Through it I learned:

  • Audiovisual production: Audio and video recording and editing, improving quality and reducing noise.
  • Social media management: Promotion on TikTok, Instagram and Twitter.
  • Content creation: Episode planning, guest outreach and promotional material.
  • Interviewing: Conducting interviews and leading conversations.
  • Community: Connecting with the competitive Spanish-speaking Smash scene.

A very enriching experience that combined my passion for video games with professional skills I would later apply to other projects.
