El Último Frame

El Último Frame is a podcast dedicated to Super Smash Bros., with interviews, monographs and experiences from the Spanish-speaking Smash community.

During the production of the podcast I developed different skills:

  • Audio and video recording and editing: Improving quality and reducing perceived noise
  • Social media management: Promoting the podcast on platforms like TikTok, Instagram and Twitter
  • Content production: Episode planning, guest outreach and promotional material creation

About the podcast

The podcast focuses on the competitive Super Smash Bros. community in Spanish, offering in-depth interviews with players, tournament organizers, content creators and other relevant figures from the scene.

Platforms:

  • YouTube
  • Spotify
  • Apple Podcasts

My first step in content creation

El Último Frame was my first content creation project on the internet. Through this podcast I learned:

  • Audiovisual production: Audio and video recording and editing, improving quality and reducing noise.
  • Social media management: Promotion on platforms like TikTok, Instagram and Twitter.
  • Content creation: Episode planning, guest outreach and promotional material.
  • Interviews: Conducting interviews and leading conversations.
  • Community: Connecting with the competitive Smash scene in Spanish.

It was a very enriching experience that combined my passion for video games with the development of professional skills that I would later apply to other projects.
