Hello

This is the rigle.dev blog. Here I’ll be sharing:

  • Practical tutorials about programming, systems and tools
  • Reflections on education and technical learning
  • Internet culture and technology
  • Useful resources that I discover and feel like sharing

Everything I publish here will be content that I think might be useful to you.

Why a blog?

The newsletter is for more direct and personal emails. The blog is for longer content, step-by-step tutorials and reflections that I want to have documented and accessible.
