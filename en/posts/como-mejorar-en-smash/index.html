How to improve at Smash?

Rodrigo Iglesias // 2021-05-30 // 99 words // 1 min

The three basic aspects to start improving.

Due to the competitive nature of these games, the topic of improvement is always on everyone’s lips. As soon as we lose in a tournament set match, our mind tells us that we need to improve something, since the other person did it better than us or we failed too many things. Playing a competitive game requires constant improvement to maintain the level.

Lately there have been some very good videos on the subject on Smash Bros Spain, but I decided to make a small introduction about what our path to improvement should be. I hope you like it!
